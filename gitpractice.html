How to make a PB&J Sandwich:

  1. Grab a jar each of peanut butter and jelly (your favorite flavor), a spreading knife, and two slices of bread.
  2. Use the knife to spread peanut butter on one slice of bread. Do the same with the jelly for the otehr slice of bread.
  3. Bring both slices together so the filling is in the middle.
  4. Take a huge bite and enjoy!
